Gem Profile

Ruby Details

Color: Deep Red. Symbolism: Passion, Prosperity.

A variety of the mineral corundum, colored by chromium.

Sapphire Details

Color: Blue (common), Pink, Yellow. Symbolism: Wisdom, Royalty.

Also a corundum variety, blue color from iron and titanium.

Emerald Details

Color: Vibrant Green. Symbolism: Hope, Renewal.

A variety of the mineral beryl, often has inclusions (jardin).

Diamond Details

Color: Colorless (prized), fancy colors. Symbolism: Love, Strength.

Hardest natural material, formed under high pressure.
